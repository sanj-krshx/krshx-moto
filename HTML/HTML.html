About Us

TVS's 300cc portfolio delivers three distinct riding experiences sharing a common racing heritage: the track-focused Apache RR 310, the aggressive Apache RTR 310 streetfighter, and the tour-ready Apache RTX 300. The full-faired RR 310 is built for high-speed stability, utilizing a 312.2cc reverse-inclined engine that puts out 38 PS and 29 Nm of torque, paired with advanced track electronics and a vertical TFT console. Stripping away the fairing, the RTR 310 channels that same performance into a sharp, urban streetfighter packed with heavy-hitting technology like a 6-axis IMU, cornering ABS, and climate-controlled seats. Meanwhile, the RTX 300 shifts focus toward long-distance exploration with a 299.1cc engine generating 36 PS, featuring upright ergonomics, handguards, an optional tall beak, and a rugged frame designed to conquer highway miles and light trails comfortably.
